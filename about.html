About Aeny Sarees

Weaving traditions, crafting excellence since 1985

Our Heritage

For over three decades, Aeny Sarees has been synonymous with elegance, quality, and tradition in the world of Indian ethnic wear. Founded by Mrs. Aeny Patel in 1985, our journey began with a vision to preserve and promote India's rich textile heritage while embracing contemporary designs.

Today, we stand as a testament to the perfect blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern innovation, with over 200 skilled artisans creating masterpieces that adorn women across the globe.

Our Values

Quality Excellence

Every saree undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our exacting standards of craftsmanship.

Artisan Welfare

We believe in fair wages, safe working conditions, and skill development for all our artisans.

Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials and processes to minimize our environmental footprint.

Customer Delight

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do, and we strive to exceed their expectations.
